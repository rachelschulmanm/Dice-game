Game instructions

  • if you turn -roll the dice (at least once) and accumulate the result in "current"
  • You can roll again or click "Hold" to save the points from "Current" and end the turn.
  • Note! if you get 6-6 you will loose all points from "current" and the turn will go to your opponent
  • if you managed to reach axactly the target score- you win! if you passed it - you loose.
